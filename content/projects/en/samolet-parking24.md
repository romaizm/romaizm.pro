## Project Overview

Samolet-Parking24 is a car parking service near Vladivostok airport. A convenient way to leave your car before a flight and pick it up when you return.

## Challenge

Create a simple, clear landing page. The main goal — explain the service benefits in plain language and help customers easily find directions to the parking lot.

## Solution

Built a clean landing page on WordPress. Minimal design, clear structure, no fluff. Integrated maps from multiple services so visitors can get directions in one click.

## Key Features

- Simple, clean design with no unnecessary elements
- Embedded maps for easy navigation
- Fast page loading
- SEO optimization for local search queries

## Results

- #1 ranking in Google and Yandex for target keywords
- High organic traffic
- Site delivered in 2 weeks
