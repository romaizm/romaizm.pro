## Project Overview

Hikor is a car import company from South Korea. The goal was to create a website for their wholesale business segment, targeting corporate clients.

## Challenge

Build a presentation website for the B2B segment that impresses potential partners and stands out from competitors. The site needed to be visually striking and convey the company's credibility.

## Solution

Built a WordPress site with a unique design crafted in Figma. Paid special attention to the visual experience — high-quality imagery, advanced animations, smooth transitions. All of this creates a premium feel, which is essential for the B2B segment.

## Key Features

- Unique design focused on B2B audience
- Advanced animations and smooth transitions
- Premium visuals matching the company's positioning
- Fully responsive design for all devices

## Results

- Company received a presentation website for working with wholesale clients
- Visually stands out among competitors in the auto import space
